CONTROLLERS & KINEMATICS
$q \in \mathbb{R}^3$ is 3D position, $\dot{q} \in \mathbb{R}^3$ is 3D linear velocity. Both measured via PX4 EKF2 (noisy). No $\ddot{q}$ measurement.
$q_d \in \mathbb{R}^3$ is the desired 3D position, $\dot{q}_d \in \mathbb{R}^3$ is desired 3D linear velocity.
Baseline controller is:

$$u(t) = \Phi(x(t), \hat{\theta}(t)) + K_P e(t) + K_D \dot{e}(t) + \int_{t_0}^t \left( K_I e(\tau) + K_{\text{RISE}} \text{sgn}(r_1(\tau)) \right) d\tau$$ 
Developed controller is:

$$u(t) = K_P e(t) + K_D \dot{e}(t) + \int_{t_0}^t \left( K_I e(\tau) + K_{\text{RISE}} \text{sgn}(r_1(\tau)) + \Phi(\kappa(\tau), \hat{\theta}(\tau)) \right) d\tau$$ 
where:

$$K_P \triangleq k_1 k_2 + k_1 k_3 + k_2 k_3 + 1$$
$$K_I \triangleq k_1 k_2 k_3 + k_1$$
$$K_D \triangleq k_1 + k_2 + k_3$$
$$\kappa \triangleq \left[ q^\top, \dot{q}^\top, q_d^\top, \dot{q}_d^\top, u^\top \right]^\top \in \mathbb{R}^{15}$$    
$$x \triangleq \left[ q^\top, \dot{q}^\top, q_d^\top, \dot{q}_d^\top \right]^\top \in \mathbb{R}^{12}$$   
$$e \triangleq q_d - q$$
$$r_1 \triangleq \dot{e} + k_1 e$$
Update law (used for both):

$$\dot{\hat{\theta}} = \text{proj}_{\mho}\left(\Gamma \left. \frac{\partial \Phi}{\partial \theta} \right|^\top r_1 - \sigma_{\text{mod}} \hat{\theta}\right)$$
$$\mho \triangleq \bar{B}_{\bar{\theta}}(0_p)$$
